FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our courses, certifications, and enrollment process.

What is CCC certification and why is it important?

CCC (Course on Computer Concepts) is a NIELIT certified program that provides basic computer literacy. It's essential for government jobs and helps you understand fundamental computer operations, internet usage, and digital services.

How long does it take to complete the courses?

Course duration varies: CCC takes 3 months, ADCA takes 12 months, and Tally ERP takes 6 months. We offer flexible timing to accommodate your schedule.

Are the certificates recognized by the government?

Yes! All our courses are NIELIT certified and recognized by the Government of India. These certificates are valid for government job applications and career advancement.

Do you provide job assistance after course completion?

Yes, we provide comprehensive job assistance including resume building, interview preparation, and connections with local employers and government opportunities.

Can I learn in my local language?

Absolutely! We provide training in Hindi and regional languages to ensure you understand concepts clearly and feel comfortable during learning.
